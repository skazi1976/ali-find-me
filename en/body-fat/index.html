How it's calculated

The calculation uses the U.S. Navy formula — it uses neck, waist (and hip for women) circumferences together with height. It's an accessible method giving a good body-fat estimate without special equipment, and more accurate than BMI because it accounts for fat.

Frequently asked questions

What is a healthy body fat percentage?

Men: athletic 6-13%, fitness 14-17%, average 18-24%. Women: athletic 14-20%, fitness 21-24%, average 25-31%.

Is the Navy method accurate?

It's a good, accessible estimate (no equipment) but less precise than DEXA. Measure correctly: neck below the larynx, waist at the navel.

How do I lower body fat?

A moderate calorie deficit + strength training to preserve muscle + high protein. Calculate your calories and protein.
