How to choose good workout leggings

Quality leggings must be squat-proof – check reviews that they aren't sheer when you bend. Look for a moisture-wicking fabric (poly-spandex or nylon) with at least 20% elastane for stretch. A high waist supports the belly and won't slide during movement. Flat seams prevent chafing. On matching sets, check that the bra and legging color truly match in buyer photos.

A full set or separates

A matching set (leggings plus bra or top) saves decisions and looks polished – great for the gym and street style. Separates give flexibility to mix colors. The strong trend: seamless sets in neutral tones – black, mocha, gray and olive. For a sports bra with medium-high support, choose a cross-back or wide straps. Always check the size chart – activewear runs fitted.

Frequently asked questions

How do I know leggings aren't sheer?

Look for buyer photos in a squat/bend and a 'squat proof' note. Thicker fabric with a high elastane content is usually more opaque.

What size for activewear?

Activewear is fitted by design. Check the size chart; if between sizes and you want support, size up for comfort.

Are seamless sets better?

Seamless sets are very comfortable, with no seams to chafe, and look polished – great for workouts and everyday wear.
