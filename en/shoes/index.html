How to buy shoes online the right way

The most common mistake is sizing. Always check the size chart for the specific model (Chinese makers often run small), and measure your foot in the evening when it's at its largest. Mind the material: quality synthetic leather looks great and lasts, but for everyday wear prefer a flexible sole and breathable lining. Read reviews with real buyer photos – they reveal the true color and quality far better than the maker's photo.

Hot shoe trends

Fashion is going back to the classics: retro sneakers (like the Adidas Samba), loafers and moccasins in leather for a clean look, and orthopedic sandals in Birkenstock style for max comfort. Leading colors: off-white, camel brown and matte black. Tip: clean white sneakers plus brown sandals cover 80% of everyday looks.

Frequently asked questions

How do I know which size to order?

Measure your foot length in cm and compare to the model's size chart (not a generic one). If you're between sizes, size up – most Chinese shoes run about half a size small.

How long does shipping take?

Usually 15-25 business days. Some sellers offer express shipping in 7-12 days – worth checking on the product page.

Can I return if the size is wrong?

Most sellers allow returns or refunds, but return shipping is pricey – better to nail the size up front.
