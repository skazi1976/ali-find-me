Which bag is right for you

The right bag depends on use: a crossbody is ideal for everyday – hands free and secure in the city. A large tote is perfect for work and moms – it holds everything. A clutch or small evening bag completes an elegant look. For a basic wardrobe, three cover almost everything: a black crossbody, a brown tote and a small evening bag.

What to check for quality

When buying online, the hardware gives away the quality: smooth zippers, straight stitching and reinforced handles. Quality synthetic (PU) leather looks and feels almost like real leather, is more water-resistant and far cheaper. Check review photos of the interior – a quality lining and inner pockets signal a good bag. Note the size in cm (not just the photo) so you aren't surprised.

Frequently asked questions

Real leather or synthetic?

Quality synthetic (PU) leather looks excellent, resists water and costs far less. For an everyday bag it's a smart choice. Real leather is luxurious but pricey and needs care.

How do I know the true size?

Don't rely on the photo – check the measurements in cm in the description and compare to a bag you already own.

Crossbody or tote for everyday?

A crossbody is better for the city and convenience (hands free, secure). A tote is better if you need to carry a lot – laptop, bottle, kids' gear.
