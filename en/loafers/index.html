Why every woman needs a pair of loafers

Loafers and moccasins are the sweet spot between comfort and elegance – flat and comfy like sneakers, yet polished like a heel. They pair with tailored trousers, jeans, a dress or a skirt, and instantly raise the look. The classics: the penny loafer, a buckle loafer, and a soft suede moccasin. The most versatile colors: black, camel brown and burgundy.

Quality and the right size

With loafers, the leather and finish give everything away – look for straight stitching, a flexible sole and a comfortable lining in review photos. Quality synthetic (PU) leather looks great and lasts. On size: loafers should sit snug but not pinch – if you're between sizes, it's usually better to size up half a size, especially if your foot is wide. A thin rubber sole gives better grip and comfort than a stiff one.

Frequently asked questions

Do loafers pinch at first?

Real, quality leather may soften over a few wears. If they pinch hard from the first wear, the size is likely too small – go up half a size.

What do I wear loafers with?

Almost anything – tailored trousers, rolled jeans, a midi dress or a skirt. They lift any casual look to elegant-comfortable.

Real leather or synthetic?

Quality synthetic (PU) leather looks excellent, is durable and cheaper. For everyday wear it's a smart, good-value choice.
