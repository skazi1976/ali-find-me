How to choose a flattering dress

The secret to a flattering dress is matching your shape: for an hourglass, a wrap dress that highlights the waist; for a pear, an A-line that flares from the waist; for a straight figure, a belted or pleated dress that creates curves. Consider length too: midi is elegant and versatile, maxi is perfect for summer comfort, and mini reads young for daytime. Always check the specific size chart, not a generic one.

Fabrics and trends for the season

For hot weather, prefer breathable fabrics – light cotton and linen, while chiffon is flattering and drapes well for events. Hot trends: floral prints, wrap silhouettes, and solid-color maxi dresses (cream, terracotta, olive). Key tip – read reviews with real customer photos to see the true color and how sheer the fabric is.

Frequently asked questions

How do I pick a dress size?

Measure your bust, waist and hips and compare to the specific model's chart. If between sizes and you want a comfortable fit, size up.

Which fabric is best for summer?

Cotton and linen are the most breathable and comfortable in heat; chiffon flatters for events. Avoid thick polyester, which can feel hot.

Maxi or midi?

Maxi is comfortable and breezy for summer and the beach. Midi is versatile – it works for both work and evening. Both flatter most shapes.
