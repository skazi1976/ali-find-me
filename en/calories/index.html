How it works

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation — the most accurate. It computes your BMR, multiplies by activity for TDEE, then adjusts for your goal. Macros emphasize high protein (2 g/kg) — ideal for training and preserving muscle while dieting.

Frequently asked questions

How many calories a day to lose weight?

A deficit of about 500 calories below your TDEE leads to a healthy loss of ~0.5 kg per week.

How much protein per day?

For training and dieting, 1.8-2.2 g of protein per kg of body weight is recommended to preserve muscle.

What's the difference between BMR and TDEE?

BMR = calories burned at rest. TDEE = total daily burn including activity. Your target is based on TDEE and your goal.
