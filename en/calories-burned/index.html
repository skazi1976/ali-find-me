How it's calculated

The calculation uses MET values (Metabolic Equivalent) — how much energy each activity requires. Formula: calories = MET × weight (kg) × hours. The more intense the activity, the higher the MET.

Frequently asked questions

How many calories does running burn?

It depends on weight and pace — a 75 kg person burns about 550 calories per hour of running. The calculator computes it exactly for your data.

Is calorie burn the same for everyone?

No — it depends on weight (more weight = more burn), intensity and fitness level. It's a general estimate.

How do I burn more calories?

Higher intensity (HIIT, running) burns more per minute. Adding weight training raises your metabolism even at rest.
