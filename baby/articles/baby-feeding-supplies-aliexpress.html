❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are silicone baby products from AliExpress food-safe?
Yes, reputable sellers on AliExpress use food-grade silicone that is BPA-free, PVC-free, and FDA or LFGB certified. Always verify the listing mentions food-grade silicone and check buyer reviews. Look for sellers with 4.5+ star ratings and thousands of orders.
When should I start baby-led weaning supplies?
Most pediatricians recommend starting baby-led weaning around 6 months when your baby can sit up independently and shows interest in food. You will need suction plates, soft silicone spoons, a waterproof bib, and a sippy or straw cup. AliExpress has starter kits with all essentials for under $10.
What is the best suction plate for toddlers on AliExpress?
Look for one-piece silicone plates with strong suction bases and divided sections. The best ones have a wide suction ring that sticks firmly to highchair trays and smooth tables. Prices range from $2.49 to $5.99 on AliExpress compared to $15-25 at baby stores.
How do I clean baby feeding supplies from AliExpress?
Most silicone feeding supplies are dishwasher-safe and can also be boiled for sterilization. Wash before first use by boiling for 3-5 minutes. For daily cleaning, warm soapy water or the top rack of your dishwasher works perfectly. Avoid abrasive scrubbers that can damage the silicone surface.
