Safe Sleep Guidelines

Always place babies on their back to sleep on a firm, flat mattress with a fitted sheet. No loose blankets, pillows, stuffed animals, or bumper pads in the crib. Use a sleep sack instead of blankets. Keep the room temperature between 68-72 degrees Fahrenheit (20-22 degrees Celsius). Remove crib mobiles once your baby can push up on hands and knees. Follow the ABCs of safe sleep: Alone, on their Back, in a Crib.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are sleep sacks from AliExpress safe for babies?
Yes, sleep sacks from AliExpress are generally safe when you choose the right ones. Look for sleeveless designs with a fitted neck and armholes that prevent the fabric from riding up over baby's face. Choose breathable cotton or muslin for summer and lightly padded versions for winter. Ensure the sack is the correct size — too large is a suffocation risk. Check for TOG ratings if listed.
What is the best white noise machine for a baby on AliExpress?
The best white noise machines on AliExpress are compact, USB-rechargeable devices with multiple sound options including white noise, rain, ocean waves, and heartbeat sounds. Look for machines with adjustable volume, auto-off timers, and clip-on designs for strollers. Prices start at $2.99 for basic models and $5-8 for feature-rich versions.
When should I stop swaddling my baby?
Stop swaddling when your baby shows signs of rolling over, typically around 2-4 months. At this point, transition to a sleep sack which allows free arm movement while still providing the cozy, contained feeling babies love. AliExpress has transition sacks with removable arm covers that make the switch gradual and easy.
Is a crib mobile necessary for baby sleep?
Crib mobiles are not strictly necessary but can be very helpful for establishing a bedtime routine. A mobile with soft music and gentle rotation signals to your baby that it is time to sleep. Remove the mobile once your baby can push up on hands and knees (around 5-6 months) as it becomes a pulling hazard. AliExpress has musical mobiles with projectors starting from $4.99.
