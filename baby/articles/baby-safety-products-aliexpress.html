Important Safety Tips

Baby-proofing is not a one-time task. Revisit your setup every few months as your child grows and reaches new areas. Test all locks and covers regularly to make sure adhesive is still strong. Get down on your hands and knees to see the world from your baby's perspective — you will spot hazards you missed standing up. Keep emergency numbers posted and store all cleaning supplies and medications on high, locked shelves.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start baby-proofing my home?
Start baby-proofing when your baby begins crawling, usually around 6-8 months. However, it is best to start at least a month before — once babies start rolling and scooting around 4-5 months, they can reach hazards surprisingly fast. Order supplies early from AliExpress to account for shipping time.
Are AliExpress baby safety products reliable?
Yes, most baby safety products on AliExpress use the same 3M adhesive and ABS plastic materials as branded alternatives. Look for products with 1,000+ orders and 4.5+ star ratings. Read reviews specifically about adhesive strength and durability. Magnetic cabinet locks and outlet covers from AliExpress work identically to name-brand versions.
What are the most important baby-proofing items?
The essential baby-proofing items are: cabinet and drawer locks for the kitchen and bathroom, corner protectors for sharp furniture edges, outlet covers for all accessible electrical outlets, door stoppers to prevent pinched fingers, and stove knob covers. A baby gate for stairs is also critical once your baby starts crawling.
Do adhesive baby locks damage furniture?
Most adhesive baby locks use 3M double-sided tape that can be removed without permanent damage. To remove, use a hair dryer to warm the adhesive for 30-60 seconds and slowly peel off. Any residue can be cleaned with rubbing alcohol or adhesive remover. Magnetic locks that mount with screws are more permanent but can be filled and painted over later.
