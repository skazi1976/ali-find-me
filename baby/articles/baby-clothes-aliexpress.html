❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What size should I order for baby clothes on AliExpress?
AliExpress baby clothes typically run small compared to US or European sizing. Always order one size up from your baby's current size. Check the seller's size chart carefully — it lists exact measurements in centimeters for height, weight, and chest. Measure your baby and compare to the chart rather than relying on age labels.
Is the fabric quality of AliExpress baby clothes good?
Yes, many AliExpress sellers offer excellent quality baby clothes in organic cotton, bamboo fiber, and premium blends. Look for listings that specify fabric composition (e.g., 100% organic cotton, 95% cotton 5% spandex). Sellers with 4.5+ stars and buyer photo reviews are the most reliable. Always wash new clothes before first wear.
How long does shipping take for baby clothes from AliExpress?
Standard free shipping takes 15-30 business days. AliExpress Choice items typically arrive in 7-12 days. Some sellers offer 5-day express shipping for a small fee. Since babies grow fast, order the next size up and plan ahead. Many parents order seasonal clothes 1-2 months in advance.
Are AliExpress baby clothes safe for newborns?
Most baby clothes from reputable AliExpress sellers are safe and made from skin-friendly materials. Choose organic cotton or bamboo for newborns as these are naturally hypoallergenic. Always wash new clothes before use to remove any manufacturing residue. Check that there are no loose buttons, rough seams, or small parts that could be a choking hazard.
