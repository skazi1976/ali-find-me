💧 Water Decals & Slide-On Transfers

Water decals are the gold standard for realistic-looking nail art. They are ultra-thin designs printed on a water-soluble backing. You simply cut out the design, soak it in water for 10-20 seconds, slide the design off the backing onto your nail, and seal with top coat. The result looks like it was hand-painted by a professional nail artist.

Application Tips for Maximum Longevity

The number-one rule for all nail stickers and decals is to always seal them with a top coat. This one step can double or triple their lifespan. For water decals, make sure the decal is completely dry before applying top coat to prevent bubbling. Trim excess material cleanly with a nail file for a professional edge. When using stickers over gel polish, cure the gel fully before applying stickers, then seal with another layer of gel top coat and cure again. Avoid submerging your hands in water for at least an hour after application.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long do nail stickers from AliExpress last?
Longevity depends on the type. Self-adhesive stickers on regular polish last 3-7 days. Water decals sealed under gel top coat can last 2-3 weeks. Full nail wraps with UV gel top coat last 1-2 weeks. Nail foils sealed under top coat last about 1 week. Always seal stickers with a top coat for maximum durability.
What is the difference between nail stickers and water decals?
Self-adhesive nail stickers have a sticky back and are pressed directly onto the nail. Water decals are printed on a thin film that is released by soaking in water for 10-20 seconds, then slid onto the nail. Water decals lay flatter and look more like hand-painted art, while stickers are quicker to apply but may be slightly thicker.
Can I use nail stickers with gel polish?
Yes! In fact, gel polish gives nail stickers the longest wear time. Apply your gel color and cure it, then place the sticker or decal, and seal with gel top coat before curing again. This sandwiches the design between gel layers and protects it for 2-3 weeks of wear.
Are nail wraps better than nail stickers?
Nail wraps cover the entire nail from cuticle to tip with a single piece, giving a smooth, seamless finish. They are great for complex designs that would be hard to achieve with individual stickers. However, they require more precise fitting and trimming. Stickers are better for accent designs on one or two nails. Both are excellent for different purposes.
