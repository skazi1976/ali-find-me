Application Tips for Best Results

Always start with clean, dehydrated nails. Push back cuticles and lightly buff the nail surface for better adhesion. Apply thin coats — two thin layers of color cure better and last longer than one thick coat. Cap the free edge of the nail with each coat to prevent chipping from the tips. Cure each layer fully under your lamp before applying the next one.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is gel nail polish from AliExpress good quality?
Yes, many AliExpress gel polishes are made in the same factories as well-known brands. Look for sellers with 4+ star ratings and check buyer photos in reviews. Popular brands like ROSALIND, UR SUGAR, and BORN PRETTY consistently deliver salon-quality results at a fraction of the price.
Do I need a UV or LED lamp for gel nail polish?
Yes, gel nail polish requires curing under a UV or LED lamp. Most modern gel polishes are compatible with both UV and LED lamps. LED lamps cure faster (30-60 seconds per coat) compared to UV lamps (2 minutes). A good starter lamp costs $5-15 on AliExpress.
How long does AliExpress gel nail polish last?
When properly applied with base coat, color, and top coat, AliExpress gel polish typically lasts 2-4 weeks without chipping. Proper nail prep (dehydrating, buffing, and using a good base coat) is key to maximum wear time.
