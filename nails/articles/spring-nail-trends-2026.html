❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are the biggest nail trends for spring 2026?
The top spring 2026 nail trends include lavender haze pastels, butter yellow, chrome French tips, glazed donut nails, 3D floral accents, micro-French manicures, aura nails with gradient effects, and soft sage green. Minimalist designs with clean lines continue to dominate alongside maximalist 3D embellishments.
What is the most popular nail color for spring 2026?
Lavender is the standout color of spring 2026, ranging from soft lilac to deeper purple tones. Butter yellow is a close second, followed by sage green, soft coral, and icy blue. Pastel shades dominate the season, often with a chrome or pearl finish for added dimension.
How can I recreate spring 2026 nail trends at home?
Most spring 2026 trends are achievable at home with affordable AliExpress products. Pastel gel polishes cost under $2. Chrome powder for glazed donut nails is under $1. Floral nail stickers and water decals replicate hand-painted designs instantly. A basic gel kit ($15-20) gives you everything needed for salon-quality spring nails.
What nail shape is trending for spring 2026?
The almond shape continues to reign as the most popular for spring 2026, offering an elegant elongating effect. Short round nails are also having a major moment for their low-maintenance appeal. Soft square (squoval) remains a versatile classic, while coffin shapes are trending for those who prefer longer, more dramatic nails.
