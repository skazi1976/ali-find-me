Safety Tips

While LED nail lamps emit only a small amount of UV light, it is still a good practice to apply sunscreen to your hands before curing or wear UV-protective fingerless gloves. Never stare directly at the LEDs while the lamp is on. Always follow the recommended cure times for your specific gel polish — over-curing can cause the gel to become brittle and crack.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What wattage nail lamp do I need?
For home use, a 48W lamp is the sweet spot — it cures all gel types in 30-60 seconds. Professional nail techs often use 80-120W for faster curing times (10-30 seconds). A 36W lamp works fine but may require longer curing. Mini 6-24W lamps are best for travel and quick single-nail touch-ups.
UV lamp vs LED lamp — which is better for gel nails?
LED lamps are better for most users. They cure faster (30-60 seconds vs 2 minutes for UV), last longer (50,000+ hours vs 1,000 hours), use less energy, and produce less heat. LED lamps work with LED and UV/LED hybrid gel polishes. Traditional UV-only gel polishes need a UV lamp, but most modern formulas are LED compatible.
Are AliExpress nail lamps safe to use?
Yes, AliExpress nail lamps are safe when used as directed. LED nail lamps emit a narrow UV wavelength that poses minimal skin risk. For extra safety, apply SPF to your hands before use or wear UV-protective fingerless gloves. Look for lamps with built-in timers and infrared motion sensors that only turn on when hands are inside.
How long does a nail lamp from AliExpress last?
LED nail lamps typically last 50,000+ hours, which means years of regular use without replacing bulbs. UV lamps may need bulb replacements every 4-6 months with frequent use. AliExpress LED lamps are so affordable ($5-15) that most users simply replace the entire unit if it ever stops working.
