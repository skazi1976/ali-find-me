Application Tips for Long-Lasting Wear

The secret to press-on nails that last is all in the prep. Start by pushing back your cuticles and gently buffing the surface of your natural nails to create texture for the glue to grip. Clean each nail with rubbing alcohol or nail dehydrator to remove all oils. Apply a thin, even layer of nail glue to both your natural nail and the press-on, then press firmly for 30 seconds. Avoid water for at least one hour after application. With this method, you can expect 1-2 weeks of flawless wear.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long do press-on nails from AliExpress last?
With proper application and nail glue, press-on nails typically last 1-2 weeks. Using adhesive tabs instead of glue gives 3-7 days of wear. Key factors include nail prep (clean, dehydrated nails), correct sizing, and avoiding excessive water exposure in the first few hours.
Are AliExpress press-on nails reusable?
Yes, most AliExpress press-on nails are reusable 2-5 times if you remove them carefully. Soak in warm water for 10-15 minutes to loosen the glue, gently peel from the sides, and clean off residual adhesive. Store them in their original box for next use.
How do I find the right size press-on nails?
Most AliExpress press-on nail sets include 24 nails in 12 sizes (2 of each). Measure your natural nail width in millimeters and compare with the size chart. If between sizes, go slightly smaller — you can file the sides for a perfect fit. Many sellers include a size guide in their listings.
What is the difference between glue-on and adhesive tab press-ons?
Nail glue provides the strongest, longest-lasting hold (1-2 weeks) but requires soaking to remove and may damage natural nails if pulled off. Adhesive tabs are gentler on nails, easy to remove, and last 3-7 days. Tabs are ideal for events or if you change your nails frequently.
