🐾 How to Choose the Right Toy for Your Dog

Not every toy suits every dog. Here's a quick guide by size and play style:

🐕‍🦺

Small Dogs (under 10 kg)

Soft plush toys, mini balls, light rope toys. Avoid hard rubber that's too big.

🦮

Medium Dogs (10-25 kg)

Standard fetch balls, medium chew bones, puzzle boards. Most versatile size.

🐕

Large Dogs (25-40 kg)

Heavy-duty rubber toys, XL rope toys, agility gear. Choose "indestructible" options.

🐻

Giant Breeds (40+ kg)

XXL chew toys, thick rope, heavy-duty tug. Always check weight ratings.

Safety Tips

Always supervise your dog with new toys, especially if they're aggressive chewers. Remove any toy that starts to fall apart. Choose toys labeled as non-toxic and BPA-free. When in doubt, size up rather than down — a toy that's too small is a choking hazard.
