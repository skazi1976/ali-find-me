Bathroom Upgrade Tips

  • Go no-drill: Self-adhesive accessories are perfect for renters and avoid damaging tiles.
  • Choose rust-proof materials: Stainless steel, aluminum, and bamboo handle humidity best.
  • Maximize vertical space: Use wall shelves, over-toilet storage, and door-back organizers.
  • Match your style: Pick a consistent color (matte black, brushed gold, or chrome) for a cohesive look.
  • Think about drainage: Shower caddies and soap dishes should have drainage holes to prevent mold.
