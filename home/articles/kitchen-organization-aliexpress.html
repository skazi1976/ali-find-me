Kitchen Organization Tips

  • Measure first: Always measure your drawers, cabinets, and shelves before ordering organizers.
  • Go vertical: Use wall space, door backs, and stacking to maximize storage in small kitchens.
  • Group similar items: Keep baking supplies, spices, and cleaning products in dedicated zones.
  • Use clear containers: Transparent bins and jars let you see what you have at a glance, reducing waste.
  • Label everything: Simple labels prevent family members from putting things back in the wrong spot.
