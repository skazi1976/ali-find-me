Smart Home Setup Tips

  • Start with smart plugs: They're the cheapest way to make any existing device smart.
  • Check WiFi compatibility: Most smart gadgets need 2.4GHz WiFi, not 5GHz. Check your router settings.
  • Pick one ecosystem: Stick with Tuya/Smart Life, Alexa, or Google Home for the best compatibility.
  • Use automations: The real power of smart home is in automated routines — lights on at sunset, coffee maker on at 7am, etc.
  • Secure your network: Change default passwords on all smart devices and keep firmware updated.
